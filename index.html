Common questions

How do the values work? PointSense estimates a realistic per-point value for each program (refreshed regularly) and multiplies it by your balance. Values are estimates for guidance, not a guarantee.

Do you connect to my bank or airline? No. We never ask for bank or airline passwords and don't link to your accounts. You add balances yourself or by forwarding statement emails.

How do I cancel Pro? Subscriptions are billed by Apple. Manage or cancel anytime in your iPhone's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.

How do I delete my data? You can delete your account in the app, or email us and we'll remove your data.
